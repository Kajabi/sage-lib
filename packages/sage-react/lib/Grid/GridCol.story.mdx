### Vertical Alignment

The vertical alignment default for each <code>.sage-col</code> aligns to stretch the full height of each respective <code>.sage-row</code> container. Alignments will only be visible if the total amount of height taken up by columns is less than the total amount of space.

Supported Types: <AlignmentTypes types={GridCol.VERTICAL_ALIGNMENT_TYPES}/>
