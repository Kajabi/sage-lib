Toolbar Editor is used for places like toolbar.

You'll need to wrap your `<ToolbarEditor />`s inside of a `<Toolbar gap={Toolbar.Gap.XS} />` to receive the desired visual treatment.

### Selected buttons
Add the `selected` attribute to your `<SageButton />`s to receive the selected treatment.
